Profile

I am a Business Intelligence & Data Systems Engineer based in Alexandria, Egypt. My work sits between data, systems, and business operations: I turn scattered files, manual processes, and disconnected workflows into structured reporting, clear dashboards, internal tools, and digital operations systems.

At Egysign, I work across BI infrastructure, SQL-based reporting, Power BI dashboards, data modeling, internal web tools, VPS-related deployment, workflow automation, and systems support. I also support new digital initiatives across the wider company ecosystem, including Lake Plaza Enterprise Square and Suncrew Energy.

My background in teaching and data training helps me explain systems clearly, document workflows, and build tools that are understandable for both technical and non-technical users.
